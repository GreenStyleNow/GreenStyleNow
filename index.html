    • Why I created this:

    My name is Gabriella Lockerman. I wanted to cultivate an enviornmentally focused blog for a few reasons. I have always been intrigued by the natural world but this became a more pronounced part of who I am due to attending the NY Youth Agriculture Leadership Conference. Interacting with people working in agriculture and enviornmental studies led me to be able to see myself in that situation much more easily and made me excited about it. I also recently took a computer science class and I wanted to practice the introductory skills I learned from it and hopefully get even better at them as this blog progresses!
